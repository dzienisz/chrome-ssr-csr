2Analyze any page

Visit any website, click the extension icon, and hit Analyze Page. The verdict appears in the popup and as a badge on the icon:

SSR
Content arrives in the initial HTML from the server. Good for SEO and first paint.
CSR
Content is built in the browser by JavaScript. Typical for SPAs and app-like sites.
MIX
Hybrid rendering — some content from the server, some hydrated or loaded client-side.

🔒 Your privacy

The extension shares anonymous usage statistics (domain, render type, performance metrics — never full URLs or personal data) to help map how the web renders. You can opt out anytime in the settings.
